Phalanx

Space Policy Wargame

Phalanx is a dashboard to view, manage, and strategize space policy. Policy makers have the opportunity to view trends, context, and other important data in order to make the right space policy-related decisions.

By ensuring decisions don't grow into conflict — Phalanx helps prevent the war before it starts.
